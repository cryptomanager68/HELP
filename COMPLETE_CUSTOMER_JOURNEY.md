**What customer does:**

5. Reviews suitability criteria for each

**What customer learns:**

- **Pathway B**: For those with limited equity
  - Join collective opportunities
  - Syndicate participation
  - Requires: interest in collective approach

  - Scale and leverage benefits
  - Risk distribution
  - Enhanced opportunities

### **FUNCTIONALITY 2: Review Strategy Frameworks** ğŸ“‹

**What customer does:**
1. Scrolls to "Strategy Framework Overview" section

**Section 1: Independent Assessment Required**
- Customer learns: Must seek professional advice

- Customer learns: All decisions are theirs

- No obligation: To participate in multiple projects
- No guarantee: Of participation
- Required: Independent evaluation for each

**Customer understands:** The rules and requirements
